Cocker Spaniel
The Cocker Spaniel is a beloved breed known for its cheerful disposition and affectionate nature. With a distinctive long, lush coat, they're recognizable for their feathered ears and expressive eyes. Energetic and playful, they thrive on human companionship and are great family pets. Originally bred for hunting, they retain a strong instinct to explore and chase. Their intelligence and trainability make them versatile in various canine activities and competitions.

West Highland
The Westie Highlander is a hybrid breed, a mix between a West Highland White Terrier and a Scottish Terrier. They typically inherit the Westie's compact, sturdy build and the Scottie's confident demeanor. With a double coat that's wiry and weather-resistant, they're well-suited to various climates. Known for their spirited personality, they're lively and courageous companions. Their loyalty and affectionate nature make them excellent family pets with a penchant for playful antics.

Shih Tzu
The Shih Tzu is a small, sturdy dog with a distinctively luxurious double coat, often flowing gracefully. Originating from ancient China, they're esteemed for their regal appearance and gentle demeanor. Their friendly and outgoing nature endears them to families and individuals alike. With a charming personality, they thrive on human companionship and are known to be great with children. Despite their royal heritage, they're playful and affectionate, making them delightful companions for all ages.

Caramelo
The Caramelo dog emerged in Brazil from crossings between mixed-breed dogs, which were common on the streets. These dogs reproduced randomly, without any control or selection. Over time, they developed similar physical and behavioral characteristics, giving rise to the breed. Caramelo has become the favorite dog breed of Brazilians, thanks to its prominence on social media, entertaining many people. They are very intelligent and loyal, capable of doing anything to please their owner.

Dalmata
Dalmatians are medium-sized dogs with a distinct spotted coat, typically black or liver on a white background. Originating from Croatia, they were historically carriage dogs and later became known as firehouse mascots. Renowned for their endurance and agility, they require ample exercise to thrive. Dalmatians are intelligent and trainable, but they may exhibit stubborn tendencies. With proper socialization, they make loyal and affectionate companions.

Yorkshire
The Yorkshire Terrier, often called Yorkie, is a small breed known for its long, silky coat that requires regular grooming. Originating from England, they were originally bred to catch rats in clothing mills. Despite their small size, they have a bold and confident personality. Yorkies are energetic and playful, making them great companions for families and individuals alike. With proper training, they can excel in various dog sports and activities.

Golden Retriever
Golden Retrievers are medium to large-sized dogs with a friendly and gentle demeanor, making them excellent family pets. They are known for their dense, water-repellent golden coat that requires regular grooming. Originally bred for retrieving waterfowl, they excel in various roles such as therapy, assistance, and search and rescue dogs. Intelligent and eager to please, they are highly trainable and adaptable to various environments. Their affectionate nature and patience with children make them beloved companions in households worldwide.
